Appliances

1. Samsung 55" Frame TV

Basic Operation:

·   Press the power button on the remote to toggle between the slide show and Smart TV options. To switch TV off, press and hold for 3 seconds.

·   Use the remote to navigate menus, select apps (Netflix, YouTube, etc.), and adjust volume.

Potential issue:

·   No Power: Check TV is on by pressing the Power Button for at least 3 seconds

·   No Signal: Ensure the correct input source is selected (HDMI, AV, etc.).

·   Wi-Fi Issues: Restart the TV and router.

 

2. Daikin Inverter Air Conditioner

Basic Operation:

·   Use the remote to turn the unit on/off (1).

Note: Settings are preset for optimal performance and do not need to be changed.

·   Press the temp arrows (2) if a cooler or hotter environment is desired

 

 

 

Potential issue:

·   Not Cooling/Heating: Ensure the unit is not set to "Fan" mode.

·   Remote Not Working: Replace the batteries or check for obstructions.  Spare batteries can be found in the cupboard under the sink.

 


 

3. Bose Smart Soundbar 300

Basic Operation:

·   Press the power button on the soundbar or use the remote to turn it on.

·   Press the TV button for use with TV.

·   Press and hold the Bluetooth button on the remote or soundbar to enter pairing mode.

·   Select "Bose Smart Soundbar 300" from your devices Bluetooth settings.

 

Potential issue:

·   Sound is coming from TV speaker: Ensure TV is selected on Soundbar. Reboot TV if required

·   Soundbar doesnt connect to mobile device: On the remote, press and hold the Bluetooth button l to make sure the soundbar is ready to connect.

On your mobile device, make sure Location Services are turned on.

On your mobile device, turn the Bluetooth feature off and then on. Delete the soundbar from the Bluetooth list. Connect again

4. Delonghi Nespresso Coffee Machine

Basic Operation:

·   Fill the water tank and turn the machine on (1 and 2).

·   Insert a Nespresso capsule and close the lever. (3 and 4)

·   Select the desired cup size (espresso or lungo) and press the button.

Potential issue:

·  No Water Flow: Ensure the water tank is filled and properly seated.

·  Capsule Stuck: Open and close the lever to eject the capsule.

 


 

5. Omega Dishwasher

Basic Operation:

·   Load dishes, add dishwashing tablet and select a wash cycle.

·   Close the door and press start.

Potential issue:

·   Not Starting: Ensure the door is securely closed.

6. Gas Cooktop

Basic Operation:

·   Select Burner.

·   Turn knob towards you (6 oclock)

·   Push knob down to ignite gas.

·   Turn knob to desired flame intensity.

 

Potential issue:

·   No Flame: Be sure knob is at desired position to allow gas to flow and push down to ignite. If does not ignite, try using matches from fireplace amenities.

 

 

7. Nectre Fireplace

Basic Operation:

·   Place firelighters or paper and dry kindling wood in the base of the firebox.

·   Open spindle for air control. (Note: Use caution: Gloves are provided as the spindle gets extremely hot after some time of use).

·   Light the paper or firelighters.

·   If necessary, leave the door slightly open as the fire establishes. Do not leave unattended with door open.

·   Once the fire has taken hold add larger pieces of wood. For optimal burn conditions, place the logs in a front to back orientation (right angles to the door opening). Too many logs may smother the fire.

·   Once the fire is established, close the door.

Potential issue:

·   Trouble starting the fire: It is best to retain some ash in the base of the firebox. A layer of ash insulates the base, helping to maintain a high temperature for combustion. Also, if all the ash has been removed from the firebox, it can affect the supply of air to the base of the fire. Be sure that the spindle is open to allow airflow.

 

 

8. DAMANI Oven

 

Basic Operation:

·   Turn the dial to select the desired cooking mode (bake, grill, etc.).

·   Set the temperature and timer.

·   Place food inside and close the door.

Potential issue:

·   Not Heating: Ensure the oven is set to the correct mode and temperature.

·   Uneven Cooking: Check the oven shelves are correctly positioned.

9. Weber BabyQ BBQ

 

Basic Operation:

·   Open the cabinet. Locate the gas bottle and open valve (1).

·   Open the gas valve (2) and ignite the burner using the ignition button (3).

·   Adjust the flame using the control knobs (2).

·   Clean the grill after use.

 

Potential issue:

·   BBQ will not light: Be sure that all gas values are switched on. Replace gas bottle with spare if gas has run out.
