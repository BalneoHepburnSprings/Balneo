Where to Eat

Lake House: A renowned fine dining destination with contemporary Australian cuisine, located beside Lake Daylesford.

Cliffy's Emporium: A charming rustic café offering hearty breakfasts, great coffee, and regional produce.

The Boathouse Restaurant: Lakeside dining with relaxed modern Australian dishes and scenic views.

Wombat Hill House: Nestled in the Botanic Gardens, this café by Lake House offers seasonal dishes and sweet treats.

Kazuki's: A stylish fusion of Japanese techniques and local ingredients, offering an intimate dining experience.

Sault: A lavender farm-based restaurant with Mediterranean-inspired meals and panoramic views.

Belvedere Social: A vibrant and modern bistro-style venue serving shared plates and classic cocktails.

Pancho: A popular local brunch spot known for creative dishes, warm atmosphere, and great coffee.
