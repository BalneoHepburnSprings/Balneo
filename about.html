THE VILLA

Nestled amid gently rolling hills just a few kilometers east of Daylesford, a charming villa in Hepburn Springs sits at the heart of Victoria’s renowned spa country, offering the perfect balance between peaceful seclusion and easy access to vibrant township life.

Surrounded by lush forested gullies, the property is bordered on one side by the leafy expanse of the Wombat State Forest, where you might spot bounding eastern grey kangaroos at dawn. To the north lie the iconic mineral springs for which the region is famous—bubbling pools of therapeutic waters still steam softly into the cool morning air—while on clear days the silhouette of nearby Mount Franklin graces the horizon.

Native birdlife abounds, from crimson rosellas flitting through the tree canopy to kookaburras perched high above, and if you stroll the winding walking tracks around the villa, you may also glimpse shy wallabies or the occasional echidna poking about among fallen logs.

Despite its proximity to Daylesford’s galleries, cafés, and weekend markets—just a ten-minute drive away—the villa feels worlds away from the bustle, tucked into its own tranquil enclave where the only sounds are rustling gum leaves and the distant murmur of natural springs.
