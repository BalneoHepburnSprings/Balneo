About

Escape to this serene hideaway, where time slows down and nature takes centre stage.

Nestled within walking distance of cafés, bars, spas, and the pampering retreats of Hepburn,
and just a few minutes down the road to the vibrant town of Daylesford, this tranquil retreat
is perfectly placed to explore local delights.

Surrounded by towering trees and the calming sounds of wildlife, it’s a sanctuary to reconnect
with your partner, with nature, and yourself.

Savour cosy moments by the crackling fire, feed the beautiful birds or venture out to discover
the joys of nearby townships.

Here, you’ll find the perfect blend of togetherness and the restorative power of solitude.
