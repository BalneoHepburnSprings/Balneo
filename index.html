Welcome to Balneo Hepburn Springs: Your Peaceful Escape

Nestled in the serene heart of Hepburn Springs, Balneo offers a truly unique accommodation experience. Just moments from the vibrant village of Daylesford, our retreat provides the perfect blend of comfort, charm, and natural beauty. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway or a tranquil break, Balneo is your ideal home away from home.

Indulge in Ultimate Relaxation & Cozy Comfort

At Balneo, every detail is designed for your relaxation. Unwind in our private mineral spa, a perfect sanctuary to soak away your worries. In the cooler months, the inviting glow and warmth of our beautiful fireplace create a truly cozy ambiance, ideal for enjoying a glass of local wine or a good book.

Explore the Nature and Charm of Hepburn Springs & Daylesford

Our prime location allows you to easily explore the renowned natural springs, walking trails, and botanical gardens that define the Hepburn Springs area. Just a short drive away, the bustling town of Daylesford awaits with its acclaimed restaurants, art galleries, and charming boutiques. Immerse yourself in the region's rich history and vibrant community, all while surrounded by breathtaking nature.
